About the Club

Our mission, vision, and what drives us

Our Mission

Finance Club empowers students to master money and build wealth through real-world skills, not just theory. We bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical finance expertise, preparing students to think like investors from day one.

We believe every student deserves access to financial literacy and the tools to build their future‚Äîwhether they're pursuing careers in finance, starting businesses, or simply managing their own wealth.

Our Vision

To become Uzbekistan's leading student finance community, where members gain the skills, network, and confidence to excel in finance careers and make informed financial decisions throughout their lives.

We envision a future where every member leaves with not just knowledge, but real connections, portfolio experience, and the edge needed to succeed in competitive finance roles.

Economics Olympiad 2026

A Free National Economics Competition for School Students in Uzbekistan

üÜì Participation is completely free of charge

Economics Olympiad 2026 is a free national economics competition open to students from public, private, and Presidential schools across Uzbekistan.

The Olympiad is jointly organized by Westminster International University in Tashkent, Economic Fundamentals Initiative (EFI), and the National Science Olympiads Center (FOM), with the aim of strengthening economic and financial literacy among school students.

Why Participate?

Who Can Participate?

  • School students aged 14 and above
  • Students from public, private, and Presidential schools
  • Languages of participation: Uzbek, Russian, or English

Academic integrity and participation rules

Academic Integrity and Responsibility

As the School Round is conducted online, teachers and school administrators are fully responsible for ensuring fair, independent, and transparent testing conditions.

Strictly Prohibited

  • ‚ùå Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools
  • ‚ùå Use of the internet, external resources, or third-party assistance
  • ‚ùå Any form of misconduct or test manipulation

Consequences of Violations

If violations are detected:

  • The student and the responsible staff member will be immediately disqualified
  • The names of the student, responsible person, and the school principal will be published on the official Olympiad website
  • The school may be banned from participating in future Olympiads

Registration Requirements

  • Each school may register only once
  • Registration must be completed by a teacher or school administrator
  • Registration deadline: March 8, 2026
